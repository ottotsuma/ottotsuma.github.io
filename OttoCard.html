I have decided to add an FAQ because I often get asked the same things.

Is it possible to get 100% normal weapon resistance? Yes, but you will be insanely weak to almost everything else, everything comes at a hidden cost. (ps. No I do not know how to do it)

Where did you get "****"? All my Items/Class/Race etc. If I choose to share and help it will be displayed on this page, if I do not reveal something, I am not shareing it.

Are Orange/Legend Items OP? This game is first of all about discovery which is why many of the stats of the items etc. I do not acctually know as they are hidden. The rating refers to how hard they are to find, I can assure you that the higher the stats or options of an item appears to be means there are really detrimental hidden affects paired with the item. To give an example, the cursed ring from the counts quest line. Stats look amazing on the display, but as all thoes that have tried to put it on know the hidden affcts are horrific. ã€€

If they are so bad why do you use them? I never said they where bad, the cursed ring is an extreme example but it is to say as long as you can work around or do not care for the downsides they are still really good items. Also this is in general, but the higher the rarity usually the better overall the item is, it really comes down to your personal prefrence.

How can I tell if my item has a hidden affect? Testing... Trial and error is the only way and alot of maths. To help you though take blue items for example. They can give a single stat boost from +0 to +40 to +80. +40 usually means it is a normal item no hidden affects. +20 for example is likely to have a positive hidden affect. +80 meanwhile will likely have a negative hidden affect. Additonally some hidden affects are shown if your detect item enchantment is high enough.

Why do you still wear the Shinra Alpha? The beta is really easy to get! Yes, while I own a beta the hidden affects while have little to zero affect on most builds, really hurt mine.

Why the System Shock ? Truthfully I do not have any of the other shock series, I am desprate to swap this for any other.

Why did you pick a class that is not suited to what you enjoy? It is not like I do not enjoy the class, even if it is focused towards PVP.

Kyber sword vs lazer sword. I get this one alot as the kyber sword is quite rare and lazer sword proforms a very similar purpose. The reduction on my class only works for the Kyber sword not the lazer sword, so that pushes me towards using it. I find the attack higher on a kyber sword but the mp drain is also higher, I am not sure what other hidden affects there are. With the 50% reduction my drain in mp works out as still more of a drain then a lazer sword.

What happens after level 100 or even 200? 100-200 is clear here that you unlock skills up to level 9 (not saying when). There are new parts of the world unlocked, pvp, events the usual, really it is about expoloring your options and the world, the quests etc you`re leveling up less often so the focus shifts alot. 200+ for me it`s speculation. All I know is somewhere from 200-300 you get level 10 skills. I am told that there is more of a focus on the non-combat stuff, not that the stuff before goes away what I mean is that the new parts are more often non-combat.
