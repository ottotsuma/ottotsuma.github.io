FEATURES

Each feature has been handcrafted with care as they are all from user feedback!
  • Character Stats

Easy Navigation

The app has easy navigation, it works similar to the in-game menu!

Stats

The stats are displayed as they are in game, it is even possible to see a break down of where they come from!

Money

The money of each currency is displayed, alongside other in-game banking options if you have an account open anywhere in the world!

Calendar

Schedule your appointments, meetings and periodical evaluations using the provided in-app calendar option

Quests

You can access your current and previously completed quest log from the mobile app as well as add and remove stared quests

Inventory

Players can not only access their inventory, but also move, sort and move to market their items

Mapping Fucntions!

After you've configured the app you can access your maps, the app allows for a few functions not found in game!

Map Functions

The map can view areas you have recorded or brought, it shows every building and names can be added!

Easy Reading

Reading is made easy with slideable text areas!

Calendar
